PII GALLERY

Philadelphia International Institute, better known as PII. Is an Eastern European and American Contemporary Gallery that displays predominantly Modern Art. It is located in the quiet neighborhood of Old City in Philadelphia, PA. The gallery is operated and owned by Margaret Berczynski. The gallery brings a much-needed array of international work including textile, printmaking, painting, sculpture and photography. With my approach towards this design, I focused on not stealing the individuality that the gallery processes while maintaining a respectable and progressive image. With the combination of pictorial and typographic logo design I achieved a logo that is approachable yet brings a certain level of prestige to the gallery as a whole. WNDR: Travel Magazine

Type WNDR is a young adult travel magazine. It is a modern approach on the seemingly repetitiveness of travel magazines. With limitations on visual stimulation you are forced to engage more with the literary elements of the magazine. This unbalanced approach to imagery and literature allows for the reader to be more entuned with the contextual ideas being shared in the magazine. The design approach is geared towards to the idea of emersion, specifically emotionally and intellectually.
