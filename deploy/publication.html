Publication
& Print Design

Project Name

WNDR: Publication Design

Objective

Develop an editorial design system for a publication based on the genre/subject matter of your chosen article. Through a series of exercises you will establish the masthead, cover system, grid system, typography system, image-making techniques and experimental layouts. Special emphasis will be placed on interpreting content through visuals and typography.

Summary

WNDR is a young adult travel magazine. It is a modern approach on the seemingly repetitiveness of travel magazines. With limitations on visual stimulation you are forced to engage more with the literary elements of the magazine. This unbalanced approach to imagery and literature allows for the reader to be more entuned with the contextual ideas being shared in the magazine. The design approach is geared towards to the idea of emersion, specifically emotionally and intellectually.
